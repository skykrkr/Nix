# Privacy Policy — Nix

**Last updated:** 2026-06-15

Nix does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Storage

All data created by Nix is stored **locally** on your device:

| Data | Storage | Purpose |
|------|---------|---------|
| Component positions, sizes, colors | `localStorage` (browser) | Remember your layout preferences |
| Background images/videos | IndexedDB (browser) | Store custom backgrounds you upload |
| Background metadata | `localStorage` (browser) | Track uploaded background list |
| Layout templates | `localStorage` (browser) | Save/load your workspace configurations |
| Volume settings | `localStorage` (browser) | Remember your audio preferences |

## No Data Transmission

- Nix does **not** communicate with any external servers.
- Nix does **not** use analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind.
- Nix does **not** inject ads or third-party content.
- Nix does **not** use cookies.

## Permissions

Nix requires **no special permissions**. It uses the `chrome_url_overrides` manifest capability solely to replace the new tab page.

## Third-Party Services

When you perform a search via Nix's search bar, the search query is sent directly to the search engine you selected (e.g., Bing, Google, Baidu). This is identical to typing a search into that engine's website directly. Nix itself does not intercept, log, or transmit this data.
